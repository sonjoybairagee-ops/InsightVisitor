Welcome! Let's get you set up
Follow these steps to start tracking visitors on your website
1
Add your website domain 30 sec
Tell us which website you want to track
Click the "+ Add domain" button in the top header → paste your website URL → click Save.

Example: https://yourwebsite.com
2
Copy your tracking snippet 10 sec
A unique one-line script will appear on your dashboard
After adding your domain, click "Copy snippet" at the top of the dashboard. You'll get a script tag like this:
<script src="https://app.visitortrack.io/pixel.js?id=YOUR_ID"></script>
3
Paste it into your website 2 min
Works with WordPress, Shopify, Wix, Webflow — any platform
Paste the snippet inside the <head> tag of your website. Choose your platform:
WordPress
Shopify
Wix
Webflow
Plain HTML
Other
4
Verify it's working instant
Visit your own website — you'll appear in the dashboard
After pasting the snippet, open your website in a new tab and visit a few pages.

Come back here — you should see yourself appear in the Visitors tab with a green "Live" indicator. That's it — tracking is active!
